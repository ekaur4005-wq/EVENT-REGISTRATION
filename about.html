Attendee Guidelines – Read This Before the Fun Begins!

Your Entry Pass Is Ready!


All registered students — check your inbox!
A special email has been sent to you containing your QR Code Entry Pass.

Just save it, show it, scan it… and walk straight into YUGANTAR like a VIP!
(Pro tip: Keep a screenshot ready for quick entry!)



1. Arrival & Entry Requirements

These document checks are ONLY for students coming by private vehicle or their own convenience:
You must carry:

  • SSP Portal screenshot or ICAI Admit Card, and
  • One valid ID proof (PAN / Aadhaar / Driving Licence)

These will be verified at the LPU entry gate — so keep them handy and avoid any holdups!

2. Movement Inside the Campus

Once you’re in, just follow the flow:

  • Stick to designated event zones
  • Avoid wandering into restricted areas
  • Be polite and cooperative with volunteers & security
    (We're here to help — not to chase you around)

3. Conduct & Prohibited Items

Let's keep the conference safe, clean and classy!

  • No political banners or slogans
  • No smoking, alcohol, or intoxicants
  • No soft drinks inside academic buildings

Bring good vibes, not trouble.

4. Personal Belongings

Your belongings = Your responsibility!
Keep your bags, gadgets, and valuables safe — organisers aren't liable for any loss.

5. Entry Cut-Off Timing

No entry after 6:00 PM
So reach on time and don’t miss the thrill!

6. Enjoy Every Bit of YUGANTAR!

Learn, network, cheer, participate, and enjoy the conference to the fullest!
Let these two days become your favourite CA memory!
